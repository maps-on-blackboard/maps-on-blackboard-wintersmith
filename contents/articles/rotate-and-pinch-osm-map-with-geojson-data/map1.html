Pinch and rotate OSM maps & GeoJSON as SVG maps using emptymap.js

OSM tiles are calculated for a given map extend with map-the-tiles and GeoJSON is rendered as SVG using geojson2svg. Map navigation (pan, pinch and rotation) achived with Hammer.js & emptymap.js.
